Hi, I'm Marissa.
I'm a UX Designer.

About Me

Coming from a background in personal training, where effective communication and personalized solutions were key, and experience in hotel management, where attention to detail and customer satisfaction were paramount, Marissa brings a unique perspective to UX design, focusing on user-centered solutions that blend functionality with creativity.

My UX Skills

UI/Visual Design

My skills encompass color theory, typography, and layout design to ensure that every element on the screen is both functional and visually appealing. By leveraging modern design tools and techniques, I aim to deliver seamless and delightful user experiences that enhance overall usability.

UX Design

Crafting intuitive and engaging user journeys through meticulous research, usability testing, and iterative design processes to ensure optimal user satisfaction and product success.

Prototyping

I create interactive prototypes that bring ideas to life, allowing for early user testing and design improvements. My prototypes range from low-fidelity wireframes to high-fidelity, interactive models, ensuring a clear vision of the final product and enabling effective communication and feedback throughout the design process.

My Work

National Security Agency Website Redesign

Our redesign of the NSA website was to enhance user experience and accessibility. We focused on modernizing the interface, improving navigation, and ensuring secure and responsive design across all devices.

Planify Travel App

Planify was designed to make booking trips seamless and personalized. The app tailors travel options to users' specific needs and preferences, providing an intuitive interface and a streamlined booking process. The result is a user-friendly experience that enhances travel planning efficiency.

North Carolina Aquarium Society Website Redesign

We redesigned the North Carolina Aquarium Society's website to improve user experience and efficiency. The new design facilitates easy access to information about the society's mission and provides a streamlined process for making donations.
